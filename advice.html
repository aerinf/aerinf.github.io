Academics

  • As a freshman, you won't take AP classes. This is totally fine.
  • Most of your teachers will always do their best to help you. Let them know if you're struggling.
  • Don't fall behind on homework - it's hard to catch up. Just set aside a little time every night to get your work done.
  • As long as you keep up with homeworks and pay attention in class, you'll be fine.
  • Start projects as soon as they're assigned. Time flies!
  • Stay organized. Tech will give you a planner - use it! Write down every homework and test.

Getting around the school

  • Memorize the directions of a compass if you haven't already.
  • Walk on the right side of hallways.
  • Use the correct staircase if it's between periods.
  • Move quickly.
  • Don't block the hallways.

Miscellaneous

  • Make as many friends as you can, in all your classes.
  • Don't worry about college yet - just join clubs or teams that you genuinely want to join. This is also a great way to make friends.
  • Take breaks for yourself and make sure you're doing things you enjoy outside of school.
  • Talk to your big sib or counselor if you need specific advice. They're happy to help!
  • The bathroom machines are usually empty, but the school nurse (4w22) always has pads if you need one.
